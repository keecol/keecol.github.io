「得」 7·30

发表于2021-07-30|更新于2021-08-01|每日总结
|阅读量:

英语

  • VW is the star performer of the motor industry this year.大众汽车在本年度汽车行业中出类拔萃。

  • judgement ①判断力；影响力 ②看法；意见；评价

    good/poor/sound judgement判断力强／弱；正确的判断力
    He achieved his aim more by luck than judgement.他达到目的主要是靠运气而不是靠判断力。

    He refused to make a judgement about the situation.他拒绝对形势作出评价。
    Who am I to pass judgement on her behaviour?(= to criticize it)我有什么资格对她的行为说三道四呢？
    I’d like to reserve judgement until I see the report.我还是想看到报告后再发表意见。（保留判断）
    It was, in her judgement, the wrong thing to do.在她看来，那样做是错误的。
    I did it against my better judgement (= although I thought it was perhaps thewrong thing to do).我这样做是违心的。

  • Don’t prolong the agony (= of not knowing sth) —just tell us who won!别卖关子了，快说谁赢了！

  • racial/social/sexual equality 种族／社会／男女平等
    equality of opportunity 机会均等
    the principle of equality before the law (= the law treats everyone the same)法律面前人人平等的原则

  • social issues/problems/reforms社会议题／问题／改革
    social class/background社会阶层／背景
    social advancement (= improving your position in society)社会地位的提高

    交际的；社交的；联谊的
    social life 社交生活
    social skill 交际能力

  • leading ①最重要的；一流的 ②（比赛中）领先的

    leading experts最杰出的专家
    leading role 主演
    He played a leading part in the negotiations.他在谈判中起到了至关重要的作用。

  • figure ①数字 ②人 ③体型

    the latest trade/sales/unemployment, etc. figures最新的贸易、销售、失业等数字
    I’m afraid I don’t have a head for figures (= I am not good at adding,etc.).恐怕我没有算术头脑。
